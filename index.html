Patriconservacy

The organization that fights for heritage sites !

Created in 1996, Patriconservacy is an organization that wishes to protect heritage sites in Europe. We are mostly based in France and in Spain. Our mission is to raise funds in order to help restore old casltes. For this reason, we need you, either you want to volunteer or to donate, you will help us reconstruct old buildings and mansions, that have suffered time.

Some of the work we have already done

  • Castilla Castle : Since 2004, we are working on Castilla Castle, in Spain, to reconstruct this 500 years old beautiful castle. At first, there were only ruins, but we are proud to say that last year we finally finished the reconstruction of the castle.
  • Blanchison Mansion : Blanchison Mansion is our newest projet. Based in France, the team in charge for its reconstruction is currently looking for volunteers to help rebuilt the mansion. But we also need funds in order to finance the reconstruction.
  • Rocary Caslte : Rocary Caslte was our first project, and thus our most significant one. After 11 years of hard work, we are proud to say that is is brand new and ready to be visited by tourists from all over the world. The caslte is based in the United Kingdom.
  • Ploudalmézeau tower : Ploudalmézeau tower, located in Brittany, France, is the oldest tower in France. This project is yet to be launched because we are looking for funds in order to finance its reconstruction. Please help us !
